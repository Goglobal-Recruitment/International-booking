Frequently asked questions

To make a booking: 1) Enter your destination and travel dates, 2) Browse available accommodations, 3) Select your preferred property, 4) Enter guest details and special requests, 5) Choose your payment method and complete the booking. You'll receive a confirmation email with your booking details and reference number.
Yes, you can cancel or modify most bookings. Go to "Manage your booking" and enter your confirmation number and email. Cancellation and modification policies vary by property and booking type. Free cancellation is available for many properties, while others may charge fees. Check your booking confirmation for specific terms.
Payment timing depends on the property and booking type. Some properties charge immediately upon booking, while others charge at the property during your stay. "Pay at property" bookings typically require a credit card to secure the reservation but charge you upon arrival. Check your booking confirmation for specific payment details.
Property contact details are available in your booking confirmation email and in the "Manage your booking" section. You can contact them for special requests, early check-in, late check-out, or any questions about facilities and services. Most properties respond within 24 hours.
You can add special requests during the booking process in the "Special requests" section, or after booking by contacting the property directly. Common requests include room preferences, early check-in, late check-out, airport transfers, and dietary requirements. Note that special requests are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.
First, contact the property directly to resolve any issues. Most problems can be solved quickly by speaking with the front desk or management. If the property cannot resolve your issue, contact our customer service team through the messaging system or phone. We're available 24/7 to help ensure your stay meets expectations.
